SWAN EARBUDS

3D Modeling Motion Design Web Development

DESCRIPTION

Swan Earbuds is a conceptual product exploring how sound, emotion, and design come together in a cohesive experience. Inspired by the elegance of the “Swan Lake” symphony, the design evokes softness, fluidity, and graceful motion — like gentle wings passing through the listener’s ears.

Combining classic simplicity with modern minimalism, the earbuds feel both timeless and contemporary. The project covers the full creative pipeline: brand direction, product concept, 3D modeling, look development, motion-based promotional assets, and an immersive website showcasing the Swan visual identity.

Visit Website

ROLE

3D Artist, Motion Designer, Web Developer

DELIVERABLES

Brand Concept & Identity System, High-Fidelity 3D Product Modeling, Cinematic Product Animation, Promotional Motion Assets, Interactive Web Experience

GOALS

1. Develop a premium product identity that communicates softness, elegance, and modern minimalism.

2. Ensure the product design visually reflects the emotional inspiration from the “Swan Lake” symphony.

3. Create cinematic motion assets that highlight key design features and strengthen storytelling.

4. Produce high-quality 3D renders and animations optimized for clarity, mood, and visual impact.

5. Build an immersive, smooth interactive website aligned with the Swan brand atmosphere and user experience goals.

CHALLENGES

1. Achieving realistic textures required precise material choices such as metallic rims, matte plastic surfaces, and soft silicone ear tips.

2. Designing the earbud form was challenging, especially shaping the curved, wing-like silhouette while keeping ergonomic comfort.

3. Balancing the swan-inspired theme to appeal to all ages and genders without leaning too feminine was essential.

4. Managing time constraints across branding, modeling, lookdev, animation, and web added significant complexity.

5. Maintaining consistent color, mood, and visual language across renders, motion assets, and the website required careful control.

Detail Shots

LEARNINGS

1. Learned how to model complex, detailed forms and set up realistic lighting and materials for high-quality product visualization.

2. Learned to create professional, advertising-style motion through camera control and clear visual storytelling.

3. Learned to integrate 3D models on the web using Model-Viewer, combining smooth scroll interactions with a structured layout.

4. Learned to manage time effectively while working across multiple disciplines throughout the full production pipeline.

RESULTS

1. Delivered high-quality 3D visuals with accurate forms, realistic lighting, and refined materials that communicate the Swan concept effectively.

2. Produced smooth, cinematic motion sequences with controlled camera movement and clear visual storytelling for promotional purposes.

3. Built a polished, responsive website featuring GSAP animations and Model-Viewer integration, offering a seamless and immersive interactive experience.

4. Achieved a cohesive brand identity with consistent mood, color, and visual language across 3D renders, motion assets, and the final website.
